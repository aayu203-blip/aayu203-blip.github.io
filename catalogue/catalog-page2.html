OUR PROFILE

Parts Trading Company (PTC) was established in 1956 by the current leadership's grandfather, with a focus on supplying critical components for the first wave of earthmoving and transport equipment entering India.

Over the past seven decades, PTC has evolved into a third-generation, multi-brand parts supplier recognised for technical accuracy, dependable stocking and longstanding industry relationships.

With deep familiarity across Volvo, Scania, Komatsu, Caterpillar and Hitachi equipment, the company has built its reputation on understanding machine systems at a granular level. This includes hydraulic assemblies, engine groups, electrical harnessing, transmission systems and undercarriage components used across a wide range of applications in construction, mining and bulk transport.

PTC operates with a structured stocking methodology, an established global sourcing network and a technical team capable of identifying the correct parts for both legacy and modern equipment. The company's approach remains consistent: minimise downtime, ensure compatibility and supply components that maintain long-term reliability.
