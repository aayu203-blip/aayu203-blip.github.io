OUR PROFILE

Parts Trading Company (PTC) was established in 1956 by the company's first-generation leadership, during a period of rapid expansion in India's earthmoving and transport sectors. From these origins, PTC has developed into a third-generation, multi-brand components supplier serving construction, mining and heavy-transport operators across the country.

Over the decades, the company has built deep technical familiarity with Volvo, Scania, Komatsu, Caterpillar and Hitachi equipment. This understanding spans hydraulic assemblies, engine groups, electrical harnessing, transmission systems and undercarriage components used across demanding field applications.

PTC operates with a structured stocking model, a dependable global sourcing network and a technical team trained to accurately identify components for both legacy and modern machinery. The company's approach remains consistent: reduce downtime, ensure compatibility and deliver components that support long-term fleet reliability.
