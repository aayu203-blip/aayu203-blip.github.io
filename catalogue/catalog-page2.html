OUR PROFILE

Parts Trading Company (PTC) was established in 1956 by the company's first-generation leadership during a period of rapid expansion in India's earthmoving and transport sectors. Over the decades, the organisation has grown into a third-generation, multi-brand components supplier serving construction, mining and heavy-transport operators across the country.

PTC's experience spans hydraulic assemblies, engine components, electrical harnessing, transmission systems and undercarriage parts used across demanding field applications. With long-standing familiarity in Volvo, Scania, Komatsu, Caterpillar and Hitachi equipment, the company is recognised for technical accuracy, structured stocking and a global sourcing capability that ensures dependable supply for both legacy and modern machinery.
