Volvo Intermediate Ring

Technical Description

Precision-engineered intermediate ring for Volvo diesel engine valve train assemblies. Functions as critical sealing interface between valve stem and guide, maintaining proper clearance under thermal expansion cycles.

Heat-resistant alloy construction withstands sustained exhaust temperatures exceeding 600°C. Prevents blow-by and maintains compression integrity. Essential for engines in heavy-duty commercial, mining, and construction applications.
