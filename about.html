Our Story

Parts Trading Company was founded in 1956 by Rasik Shah at Grant Road, Mumbai — a time when heavy earthmoving equipment was transforming India's infrastructure. Rasik recognised early that fleet operators needed a dependable local source for spare parts, not just what the OEM catalogues offered.

What began as a small parts trading operation grew steadily over the decades. As India's construction, mining, and logistics sectors expanded, so did PTC's inventory, brand coverage, and reach.

Today the company is run by Rasik's sons — Lalit Shah, Vinesh Shah, and Sanjeev Shah — each with over 40 years of hands-on experience in heavy equipment parts. Their combined knowledge covers virtually every major brand: from Volvo and Scania trucks to Komatsu and Caterpillar excavators, from Hitachi mining machines to underground drilling equipment.

The third generation of the Shah family is now actively involved, carrying forward the same commitment to deep product knowledge and fast, reliable supply.

70+ Years of Supply

1956

Founded by Rasik Shah, Grant Road Mumbai

Rasik Shah establishes Parts Trading Company as a spare parts supplier serving Mumbai's growing fleet of imported heavy equipment.

1980s – 1990s

Expansion into major OEM brands

As European and Japanese brands — Volvo, Scania, Komatsu — become dominant in Indian construction and mining, PTC expands its range to cover these marques in depth.

2000s

Second generation takes the helm

Lalit Shah, Vinesh Shah, and Sanjeev Shah — each with deep parts expertise built over decades — take over operations. The company grows to cover 22 brands and 18,000+ parts.

2010s

International export operations

PTC begins exporting parts to Russia, Africa, Indonesia, UAE, and Australia — serving mining and construction operators worldwide who rely on the same equipment brands.

Today

Third generation joins the business

The third generation of the Shah family is now actively involved, bringing with them modern systems while maintaining the personal, expert-first approach that has defined PTC for 70 years.

OEM vs Aftermarket

We supply both genuine OEM parts and OEM-specification aftermarket alternatives. Our specialists will advise on the right choice for your application — whether that means brand-new OEM for warranty-sensitive repairs, or a quality aftermarket equivalent that offers better value for older machines.
