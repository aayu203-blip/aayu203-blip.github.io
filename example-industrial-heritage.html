TECHNICAL OVERVIEW

Precision-machined intermediate ring designed for Volvo diesel engine valve train assemblies. Functions as a critical sealing interface between valve stem and guide, maintaining proper clearance under thermal expansion cycles.

Heat-resistant alloy construction withstands sustained exhaust gas temperatures exceeding 600°C. Prevents blow-by and maintains compression integrity across extended service intervals. Essential component for engines operating in heavy-duty commercial, mining, and construction applications.
