Hitachi Excavator Parts in India: ZX200, ZX300 Sourcing & Maintenance Guide

Published: 2026-03-17 • Parts Trading Company, Mumbai

Hitachi excavators — particularly the Zaxis ZX200 and ZX300 series — are workhorses on Indian infrastructure sites. Their reliability is excellent, but when parts are needed, sourcing them quickly can be challenging.

Hitachi Excavators in India: Overview

Hitachi Construction Machinery India operates through dealerships primarily in Maharashtra, Gujarat, Karnataka, and Tamil Nadu. Their India operations focus on equipment sales, with parts availability sometimes lagging behind machine penetration in newer markets like north-eastern India.

Most Demanded Hitachi Parts

Hydraulic main pump seals top the replacement list for Indian Hitachi operators. The ZX200-5A's HPV102 pump seals fail faster in India's high-temperature environment.

Swing reduction gear oil seals are the second most common replacement. Swing mechanism seal kits should be changed every 2,000 hours as a preventive measure.

Arm and bucket cylinder seal kits — The dusty environments in Indian quarries and construction sites accelerate cylinder seal wear significantly.

How to Cross-Reference Hitachi Parts

Hitachi part numbers follow a 9XXXXXXX format (e.g., 9172744). Always provide the machine serial number (starts with ZX) for accurate cross-referencing.
