Epiroc & Sandvik Rock Drifter Parts in India: Complete Sourcing Guide

Published: 2026-02-12 • Parts Trading Company, Mumbai

India's underground mining industry—particularly in the gold mines of Karnataka, the coal mines of Jharkhand, and the iron ore mines of Odisha—is largely powered by Epiroc and Sandvik rock drilling equipment. These machines take extreme punishment, and their parts are among the most technically demanding in the mining industry.

Key Rock Drifter Models Used in India

Epiroc

  • COP 1838 — High-frequency drifter for underground development drilling
  • COP 2550 — Heavy-duty drifter for production drilling
  • COP 4050 — Large-hole drifter for shaft sinking operations

Sandvik

  • RD525 — Surface and underground drifter
  • HLX5 — High-frequency drifter used across Indian metalliferous mines
  • HL710 — Handheld rock drill commonly used in narrow vein mines
