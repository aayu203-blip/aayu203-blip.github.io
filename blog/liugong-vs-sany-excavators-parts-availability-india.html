LiuGong vs SANY Excavators in India: Which Has Better Parts Availability?

Published: 2026-03-13 • Parts Trading Company, Mumbai

Chinese equipment brands have made major inroads in India's construction sector, with LiuGong and SANY now among the top-selling excavator brands. But fleet managers consistently ask the same question: which brand's parts are easier to find?

Market Presence in India

SANY entered India in 2003 and has a wider dealer network with ~120+ touchpoints across the country. Their SY215 and SY305 series dominate Indian infrastructure projects. SANY India maintains a parts warehouse in Pune.

LiuGong's India presence is newer but growing. Their 922E and 950E models are gaining traction in quarrying and mining. LiuGong's India assembly plant in Pithampur (MP) has helped improve local parts supply.

Verdict

For fleet operators where uptime is critical, SANY currently has the edge in India for parts availability. However, for models that share common Cummins or Volvo Penta engines, both brands benefit from the wider generic parts ecosystem.
