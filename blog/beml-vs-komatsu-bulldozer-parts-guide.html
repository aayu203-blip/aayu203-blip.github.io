BEML vs Komatsu Bulldozer Parts: Interchangeability & Sourcing Guide India

Published: 2026-03-10 • Parts Trading Company, Mumbai

BEML (Bharat Earth Movers Limited) has long partnered with Komatsu to manufacture bulldozers in India, which creates an interesting parts compatibility question: can BEML bulldozer parts be replaced with off-the-shelf Komatsu parts?

The BEML-Komatsu Partnership Explained

BEML manufactures bulldozers at its Mysuru plant under licence from Komatsu. Models like the BEML BH120 and BEML BH80 share significant DNA with the Komatsu D155A and D85 series. However, localisation for Indian manufacturing means that not all parts are identical.

How to Identify the Right Part Number

Always quote the BEML Equipment Serial Number and Model Number when ordering parts. Your BEML machine plate will show a plate which references the Komatsu base model. Use that Komatsu model/part number when ordering from specialists like Parts Trading Company.
