Heavy Earthmoving and Mining Machinery

Devshi Motors Private Limited stands at the forefront of the Heavy Earthmoving and Mining Machinery (HEMM) industry, setting benchmarks for excellence and reliability in parts supply. With a legacy of innovation and a commitment to quality, we are a trusted name for mining and construction businesses across India.

Operating through eight strategically located depots in key mining hubs, Devshi Motors Private Limited ensures seamless availability and swift delivery of critical components. As a leading importer and distributor, we specialize in providing a comprehensive range of parts for excavators, dozers, loaders, and mining & Off-Highway trucks.

Our extensive inventory includes undercarriage, engine, hydraulic, transmission, gear box, chassis, and electrical parts, tailored for renowned machinery brands such as Volvo, Scania, Liebherr, Komatsu, Hyundai, Caterpillar, and LGMG.

Customers can choose from OEM-certified parts or high-quality IAM (Independent Aftermarket) alternatives, ensuring flexibility and value for every need.

At Devshi Motors Private Limited, our mission is to empower the mining and earthmoving sectors with reliable, durable, and cost-effective solutions. Whether you seek to maintain operational efficiency or minimize downtime, our expertise and customer-centric approach make us your ideal partner in progress.
